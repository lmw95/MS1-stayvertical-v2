About us

Stay Vertical is founded on the shoulders of an incredible team, lead by Lead Instructor Aoife McNeal. Aoife worked in the adult entertainment industry for 10 years before making her transition into instructing. After 3 years of teaching, she and her husband Andrew opened Stay Vertical (with just two poles!) which welcomed its first students through its doors in 2012. Since then, the studio has expanded to include aerial hoop, a reinforced floor and a and a dedicated area to stretching and S&C equipment.
